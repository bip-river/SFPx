    Troubleshooting
    Payment and permit issues
    I paid, but didnâ€™t receive my permit

    After completing payment, download your permit from the confirmation page. A copy is also sent to the email address you provided. If you do not see the email, check your spam or junk folder. Email delivery may take several minutes.

    I closed the page or lost my permit after paying

    This system does not support online permit lookup. If you cannot locate your permit or confirmation email, contact your local BLM office during business hours. Staff can help locate your permit using your name and email address.

    My payment was declined or I received an error

    Payment errors may occur due to bank restrictions, session timeouts, or temporary Pay.gov issues. Verify your payment information and try again. If the problem continues, wait a few minutes before retrying or contact your bank for more information.

    I think I was charged twice

    If you believe you were charged more than once, contact your local BLM office during business hours. Be prepared to provide your name, email address, and any payment confirmation information you received.
